# Economics of AI Spend

## 💸 The Problem: The "Pro-Plan" Leak
Medium-sized startups (50-200 people) often have decentralized AI spend. Designers buy Midjourney, Engineers buy Cursor, and Marketing buys Jasper. Without a central audit, companies pay 30% more than needed.

## 🧮 Sample Calculation
- **Scenario**: 50 Engineers using ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) + Cursor Pro ($20/mo).
- **Total**: $2,000/mo.
- **Audit Findings**: 15 engineers have dormant Cursor seats; 10 users overlap with a team Claude plan already paid for by DevOps.
- **Identified Waste**: $500/mo ($6,000/yr).

## 💎 Value Creation (Credex)
Credex acts as the **CFO for the AI Era**. 
1. **Consolidation**: Moving individual seats to a negotiated Team/Enterprise plan.
2. **Credits**: Accessing "Startup Packs" and cloud credits that the customer might not know they qualify for.
3. **Usage Tuning**: Identifying users who are on high-tier plans but only use low-tier features.

## 💰 Revenue Model
- **Basic Audit**: Free (Lead Gen).
- **Pro Dashboard**: $99/mo for ongoing monitoring.
- **Enterprise / Concierge**: Success-based fee (20% of the first 12 months of actual saved spend).
