# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 🎯 Target Audience
1. **AI-First Startups**: High tool usage, fast scaling, low spend oversight.
2. **Global Dev Agencies**: Large teams using multiple coding assistants (Cursor, Copilot, Tabnine).
3. **CFOs/Finance Teams**: Looking for automated ways to cut OpEx without hurting productivity.

## 🚩 Pain Points
- "I don't know how many ChatGPT seats we actually have."
- "My bill for Cursor just doubled and I don't know why."
- "The design team just bought another AI tool we already have an enterprise plan for."

## 📣 Positioning
*"The Single Source of Truth for your AI Tool Stack."*

## 🚀 Launch Strategy
1. **Product Hunt**: Launching the "Free Savings Calculator" as a viral hook.
2. **LinkedIn Content**: Sharing "benchmark reports" (e.g., "The State of AI Spend in 2024") to build authority.
3. **Cold Outreach**: Targeting CTOs of Series A/B startups with "Estimated Savings" based on their publicly listed tech stacks.

## ✅ Success Metrics
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of users who run an audit and then book a call.
- **Referral Rate**: "Invite a CFO" feature tracking.
- **Total Savings Identified**: A public counter on the landing page to build social proof.
