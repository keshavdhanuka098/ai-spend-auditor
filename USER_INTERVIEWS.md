# User Research & Interviews

## Persona 1: The Startup Founder (Alex)
- **Role**: CEO of a 20-person Fintech startup.
- **Stack**: ChatGPT Team, Midjourney, Claude Pro.
- **Spend**: ~$800/mo.
- **Pain Point**: "I have no idea who is using what. I just see 'OpenAI' on my card every month."
- **Desired Feature**: "A single dashboard that shows me active vs. inactive users."

## Persona 2: The Freelance Creative (Jordan)
- **Role**: Senior UI/UX Designer.
- **Stack**: Jasper, Midjourney, Photoshop AI, ChatGPT.
- **Spend**: ~$150/mo.
- **Pain Point**: "Individually they are cheap, but together they cost more than my rent's utility bill. I want a bundle."
- **Desired Feature**: "Recommendations for cheaper all-in-one alternatives."

## Persona 3: Engineering Manager (Casey)
- **Role**: Head of Dev, 100+ Engineers.
- **Stack**: Cursor Enterprise, GitHub Copilot, Tabnine.
- **Spend**: ~$4,000/mo.
- **Pain Point**: "We have massive overlap. Some teams love Cursor, some prefer Copilot. We are essentially paying for two coding assistants per dev."
- **Desired Feature**: "Benchmarking against other large engineering orgs to see what the 'standard' stack is."
